It is important to get 7-8 hours of sleep

Things to do before bed

1)have a set bedtime

2)try to make your room as dark as possible

3)keep the temperature cold but comfertable

The 3-2-1 Rule

The 3-2-1 Rule tells you what to do before you go to sleep

3

The 3 means no food or drinks except water

2

The means no work or school 2 hours before bedtime these things cause stress and distract making it harder for you to sleep

1

The 1 means means no screen-time 1 hour before going to bed use this time to read a book or something else relaxing

On the bed

If you can't sleep try this simple exercise

step-1) In your head repeat a affirmation like I am feeling sleepy or visualize your dreams and goals

step-2) from your head to your toes relax your muscles

Step-3) It won't work immediately but keep reapeating your selected affirmation
